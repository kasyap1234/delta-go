# Initial Concept
A high-performance trading bot for Delta Exchange India designed to generate profits in live markets through automated execution of various trading strategies.

# Product Definition

## Target Users
- Individual Quant Traders seeking automated alpha in the cryptocurrency markets.

## Goals
- Primary Objective: Generate consistent profits in live trading environments.
- Maximize risk-adjusted returns through robust strategy execution and automated risk management.

## Core Features
- **Robust Execution Strategies:** Implementation of diverse trading strategies including Scalping, Grid Trading, and Funding Arbitrage.
- **Structural Bot Components:** Reliable high-frequency infrastructure with real-time WebSocket data feeds and efficient order management systems.
- **Integrated Backtesting:** A framework for validating strategy parameters against historical data to ensure viability before live deployment.

## Performance & Reliability
- **High Availability:** Designed for maximum uptime with automatic reconnection logic and state recovery mechanisms.
- **Low Latency:** Optimized for low-latency order execution and real-time risk validation to minimize slippage and manage exposure.
- **Observability:** Comprehensive logging and performance monitoring to facilitate detailed post-trade analysis and system health tracking.

## Constraints & Requirements
- **Delta Exchange India Compatibility:** All implemented strategies and execution logic must be fully compatible with and available on the Delta Exchange India platform.
- **Operational Safety:** Strict adherence to exchange API rate limits and safety protocols to protect capital and maintain account standing.
